Featured Projects

Accordion Component Accessibility & Redesign

I tackled the challenge of making hierarchical content intuitive and inclusive across Safari, Zotero, and VS Code. My audience included keyboard and screen-reader users navigating complex lists. In early iterations I compared native details/summary elements with custom ARIA patterns, weighing simplicity against styling flexibility. The final design combines semantic markup, clear focus states, and “Show all/Hide all” controls, demonstrating how iterative testing and semantic design improve both usability and accessibility.

Personas & Storyboarding

I conducted contextual observations and interviews at a self-checkout kiosk in Whole Foods to understand user goals and frustrations. My audience comprised everyday shoppers and UX researchers looking to streamline self-service workflows. Iterations refined my interview scripts and storyboard frames to spotlight pain points such as scanner errors and unclear item labels. My findings underscore the power of empathy-driven storytelling to guide design decisions and humanize automated experiences.

Responsive Redesign for Quora

I reimagined a Quora Q&A page to enhance content hierarchy and readability across devices. My audience, knowledge seekers frustrated by mixed content and hidden ads, guided my layout choices. I created mobile, tablet, and desktop mockups balancing whitespace, typographic scale, and subtle signifiers for sponsored versus related content. The hi-fi prototype, built with Bootstrap, shows how deliberate spacing and semantic styling can reduce cognitive load and foster a focused reading experience.
